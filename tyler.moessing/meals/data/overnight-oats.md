## Ingredients
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup milk of choice
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1 banana, sliced

## Instructions
1. Stir the oats, milk, yogurt, peanut butter, syrup, and vanilla together in a jar.
2. Fold in half the banana slices.
3. Seal and refrigerate **overnight** (at least 4 hours).
4. In the morning, top with the remaining banana and eat cold.

## Notes
Divide between two jars before chilling for grab-and-go portions.

## Tips
- Too thick? Splash in more milk. Too thin? Add a spoon of oats.
- Warm it 60 seconds in the microwave if you prefer it hot.

## Variations
- Swap peanut butter for almond butter and banana for berries.
- Add chia seeds for extra thickness and omega-3s.
