## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb boneless chicken thighs, cubed
- 2 cups cooked white or brown rice
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 3 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp water (slurry)
- Sesame seeds and green onion to garnish

## Instructions
1. Whisk soy sauce, honey, vinegar, ginger, and garlic to make the teriyaki sauce.
2. Sear the chicken in a hot pan until browned and cooked through, ~8–10 minutes.
3. Pour in the sauce and simmer 2 minutes.
4. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook 1 minute until glossy and thick.
5. Steam the broccoli until crisp-tender.
6. Build bowls: rice, chicken, broccoli. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onion.

## Notes
Thighs stay juicier than breasts for meal prep, but either works.

## Tips
- Portion into 4 containers; keeps 4 days in the fridge.
- Keep sauce slightly loose — it thickens as it cools.

## Variations
- Add edamame, carrots, or snap peas.
- Swap chicken for tofu or salmon.
