## Ingredients
- 6 large flour tortillas
- 10 eggs, beaten
- 1 lb breakfast sausage
- 1 cup shredded cheddar
- 1 diced bell pepper
- 1/2 diced onion
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Hot sauce or salsa, optional

## Instructions
1. Brown the sausage in a skillet, breaking it up. Remove and set aside.
2. Soften the onion and pepper in the sausage fat, ~4 minutes.
3. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble softly. Fold the sausage back in.
4. Warm the tortillas so they roll without cracking.
5. Fill each with eggs, sausage, and cheese. Fold in the sides and roll tight.
6. Serve now, or wrap individually in foil and freeze.

## Notes
To reheat from frozen: unwrap, microwave 2–2.5 minutes, flipping halfway.

## Tips
- Let the filling cool before rolling if you're freezing — less sogginess.
- A thin layer of cheese against the tortilla acts like glue.

## Variations
- Swap sausage for chorizo, bacon, or black beans (vegetarian).
- Add cooked hash-brown shreds for a heartier burrito.
