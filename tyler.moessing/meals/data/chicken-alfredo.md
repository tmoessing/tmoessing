## Ingredients
- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 8 oz fettuccine
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup grated parmesan
- Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions
1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
2. Sear in a hot pan with a little oil, ~5–6 minutes per side, until 165°F. Rest, then slice.
3. Cook the fettuccine in salted water until al dente. Reserve **1/2 cup pasta water** before draining.
4. In the same pan, melt butter and cook the garlic 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Add cream, bring to a gentle simmer, then whisk in the parmesan until smooth.
6. Loosen the sauce with pasta water as needed, toss in the pasta, and top with sliced chicken.

## Notes
Grate the parmesan fresh — pre-shredded cheese has anti-caking agents that make the sauce grainy.

## Tips
- Don't let the cream boil hard or it can split.
- Add a pinch of nutmeg for a classic alfredo depth.

## Variations
- Swap chicken for shrimp (cook just 2–3 minutes).
- Stir in steamed broccoli or peas for a one-bowl meal.
